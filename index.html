Hi! My name is Chris, and I recently graduated from Stanford University with a PhD in Biology.

My research focused on understanding the coordination of biosynthesis with cell size. I used a suite of modern omics techniques and high-throughput confocal microscopy of nascent mRNA to discover that cells adjust their transcription with increase in cell size.

Before my PhD, I was a research assistant at Rockefeller University where I worked in collaboration with Ali Brivanlou and Eric Siggia's members to understand early human development using embryo models.
